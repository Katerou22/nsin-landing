What is nsin?

nsin is a connectivity-focused CDN designed to solve one big problem: making sure your website works everywhere. Whether your server is inside Iran or abroad, we route your visitors through our edge so they can always reach your site — fast, simple, and without barriers.

Global & Local Reach

From Iran to the rest of the world and back, nsin keeps your website accessible — no matter where it's hosted.

Connectivity First

We focus on solving access issues. Traffic is passthrough from our edge to your origin — not caching, not security, just rock-solid reachability.

Simple & Reliable

No extra setup or complexity — just a clear promise: your users will be able to reach your website from anywhere.

SEO Solver

Search engine crawlers like Googlebot will always be able to reach your website. Even during severe connectivity issues, nsin ensures crawlers see your site as online — protecting your indexing, rankings, and SEO health.

Always-On SSL

Your website stays protected with SSL at all times. Even if your origin server becomes unreachable, nsin continues to serve HTTPS securely from the edge — no certificate errors, no downtime warnings.

Zero Configuration

No code changes, no complex setup. Point traffic to nsin and your site becomes globally reachable with built-in resilience.
